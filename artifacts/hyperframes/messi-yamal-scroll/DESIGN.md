# CHUTE Scroll Hero

## Style Prompt

Cinematic football confrontation with Neo Terminal discipline: black stadium atmosphere, restrained national colors, fluorescent lime used only for decisive motion, oversized athletes, sharp editorial framing, and enough negative space for the website interface.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#080B0A`
- Primary text: `#F1F4EC`
- CHUTE lime: `#BAFF00`
- Argentina light: `#75A8E8`
- Spain red: `#D42B32`

## Typography

- Display: `Barlow Condensed`, 800–900
- Data: `IBM Plex Mono`, 500–600

## Motion

- Arc: `SLOW → BUILD → REVEAL → REFLECT`
- One continuous camera push; no jump cuts.
- Entrances are asymmetric and use varied easing.
- Ending holds a useful hero frame for scroll pause.

## What NOT to Do

- No decorative gradients covering faces.
- No score cards, navigation, CTA, narration, or duplicate interface copy.
- No infinite animation loops.
- No fabricated live-stat claims.
